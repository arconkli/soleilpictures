Soleil Clusters

Soleil Clusters is a creative workspace and moodboard tool for film, photo, design, and brand teams — organize references, projects, and ideas in one place, and collaborate with your team in real time.

  • Live canvas — your whole team on one infinite canvas, with live cursors, comments, and presence.
  • Auto-tag — drop any image, link, or file and Clusters reads it, tags it, and files it to the right board.
  • Docs, built in — write briefs right beside the canvas with slash commands and @mentions.
  • Share or lock — share a board with one link, or keep it private. You own your references.
  • Relationship graph — every card connects; jump between boards through a living graph of how your ideas link up.
